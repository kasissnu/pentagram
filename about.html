About the studio

Interiors that balance polish with personal ease.

Pentagram Interiors is a Gurgaon-based studio for homeowners who want premium spaces without visual noise. Our work is measured, tactile, and deeply attentive to daily life.

Our philosophy

Luxury is in proportion, light, material, and restraint.

We begin with how a family moves through the home, then layer mood, furniture, finishes, lighting, art, and storage around that rhythm. The result is sophisticated, but never stiff.

Lead designer

Design direction with a calm, decisive hand.

Pentagram Interiors is built around a simple belief: luxury should feel quiet, not loud. That shows up in the way we compose room proportions, simplify circulation, and choose finishes that look better as the years pass.

The studio works closely with homeowners, contractors, and artisans so each project stays coherent from concept through site completion.
